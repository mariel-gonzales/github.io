About Me

I study music perception, illusion perception, and multisensory processing at UC Merced. I'm also a baker and love to experiment in the kitchen and try out new bakeries. When I'm not doing those things, I'm usually watching a movie, playing the piano (or trying to), or cuddling my cat.


This website will be my primary medium for sharing my work and some of the things I care about.
