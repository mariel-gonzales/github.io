About

I'm a PhD candidate at the University of California, Merced, in the Cognitive & Information Sciences department where I study music perception, illusion perception, and multisensory processing. I'm also a baker who loves experimenting in the kitchen and trying new desserts. When I'm not doing those things, I'm usually watching a movie, playing the piano (trying to), or cuddling my cat.
